Sourdough Bread

Description

Apparently this recipe is award-winning!

Got this recipe from allrecipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons wheat germ
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sourdough starter
  • 2 tablespoons margarine, softened
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon cornmeal

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bread machine using a Manual cycle. That's usually two mix cycles approximately 15 to 20 minutes with teh rise cycle between them of about an hour.
  2. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface. Form the dough into a single round loaf. Place the loaf on a baking sheet which has been lightly oiled and prinkled with cornmeal. Cover loaf and let rise in a warm place until nearly doubled in size, about 40 minutes.
  3. Prehead oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes, until golden brown and loaf sounds hollow then tapped. Let cool on a wire rack
